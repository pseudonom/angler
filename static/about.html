This is a quick experiment based on the oft-heard writing advice that one should prefer Anglo-Saxon words to Latinate words (for example, Orwell's Politics and the English language).

I tried keep the illuminated manuscript design historically accurate. Sources include:
  • Wikipedia—Illuminated Manuscript
  • Online Catalogue of Illuminated Manuscripts
  • Simple Drawn and Painted Borders
The typeface is off by several centuries. It's a concession to legibility while attempting to remain evocative.
The source code is available on Github.
